Essential Digital Wellness Practices

Morning Screen Freedom

Start your day with at least one hour of screen-free time. This allows your brain to wake up naturally, reduces stress hormones, and sets a calm tone for your day. Try meditation, journaling, or a short walk instead.

Eye-Protecting Night Mode

Activate night light filters after 8 PM to reduce blue light exposure. This helps maintain your natural circadian rhythm and improves sleep quality by supporting melatonin production.

Phone-Free Sleep Sanctuary

Use a traditional alarm clock instead of your phone. Keeping your phone outside the bedroom eliminates the temptation for late-night scrolling and creates a true tech-free relaxation space.

Pro Tip: During meals and breaks, keep your phone in another room or at least out of sight. Research shows this simple practice improves digestion, social connection quality, and mental refreshment.
